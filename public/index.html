Who we are

A private UAE government-services partner, in your corner

ZOOM Government Services is a private service provider — not a government department. We prepare, submit and follow up your applications with the relevant UAE authorities and systems, including GDRFA, ICP, MOHRE, DET, MOFA, DHA and the FTA, so you don't have to navigate them yourself.

From a single visa to a fully licensed company, our team handles the documents, the typing, the submissions and the follow-up — with clear pricing and live status updates from start to finish.
