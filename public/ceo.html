From the founder

Welcome to ZOOM

When we started this company, we set out to solve a problem almost everyone in the UAE knows too well: government paperwork is complicated, time-consuming and stressful. Visas, Emirates IDs, trade licences, approvals — each one its own maze of forms, departments and queues. We believed there had to be a simpler way, so we built it.

Today, ZOOM brings every government service under one roof. Our role is simple: to take the complexity off your shoulders and handle it for you — accurately, quickly, and right the first time. Behind every application is a team of specialists who know the requirements inside out, treat your documents with care, and stay with you from the first step to final approval.

What sets us apart isn't only expertise — it's trust. Individuals, families and businesses across Dubai rely on us with something they can't afford to get wrong: their official affairs. We don't take that lightly. Every client and every transaction is handled with the same commitment to honesty, speed and service.

As the UAE continues to lead the world in innovation and ease of doing business, ZOOM is proud to grow alongside it — investing in technology that lets you complete more services online, while keeping the personal, face-to-face support our customers value.

Thank you for placing your trust in us. Whatever you need, we'll make it happen.

Zaib ur Rehman
Founder, ZOOM Government Services
